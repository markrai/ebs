Access modifiers (or visibility modifiers) are applied to 
classes, inner-classes, fields (variables), and methods.

CLASSES:
 	can only be [default] and public.

INNER CLASSES:
	can only be public, [default], & private.

FIELDS (variables):
	can be public, [default], protected, or private.

METHODS:
 	can be public, [default], protected, or private.


From least restrictive to most restrictive:

	 public 
	 [default]
	 protected
	 private
