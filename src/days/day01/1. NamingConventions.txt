projects - all lower case

packages - all lower case
           often in the form of "com.companyname.module"
           
class    - CamelCase
           e.g. "Person," "TextSearch," or "LargeTextFormatter"

variables - first letter lower-case, but camelCase.
            example: "myNumber," "placeholder," or "firstName"

methods - first letter lower case, but camelCase.
          example: screenSearch();
